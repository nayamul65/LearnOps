# LearnOps — Design Guidelines

## Stance
Minimalist-premium SaaS with educational warmth. Green (#22C55E) is the single accent. Generous whitespace, crisp type hierarchy, soft shadows. Trustworthy for Bangladeshi parents, sharp for business owners.

## Typography
- Headings: Poppins SemiBold (600)
- Body: Poppins Regular (400)
- Bengali: Hind Siliguri (400/600)

## Color
- Primary: #22C55E | Primary Dark: #15803D
- Light bg: #FFFFFF | Dark bg: #0F172A
- Card light: #F8FAFC | Card dark: #1E293B
- Muted text: #64748B | Border: rgba(0,0,0,0.08)

## Shape & Spacing
- Radius: 1rem cards, full pills
- Section padding: py-20 to py-24
- One primary CTA per section

## Rules
- Bengali text uses Hind Siliguri only
- Dark mode: slate-900 ground, slate-800 cards
- Stats in Poppins Bold for visual weight
